LCMS

    What: Annual vegetation cover change, land cover, and land use data

    When: 1985 to present

    Where: Across conterminous U.S., SE Alaska, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, Hawaii

APPLICATIONS

Monitor annual land cover changes

LCMS is a useful tool for monitoring landscape changes caused by natural disturbance regimes and human activies

  • When and where change occurs
  • Vegetation change - tree mortality
  • Urban vegetated and non-vegetated change - urban expansion
  • Water body change - reservoir water loss
  • Cryosphere change - glacial retreat
  • Change attribution - wildfire, harvest, hurricane, insect and disease
  • Where were trees and are they still there?
  • Where was there water and is it still there?
  • Where was there snow or ice and is it still there?
