Welcome to the Landscape Change Monitoring System (LCMS) in Motion - Data Animation Explorer!

LCMS is a landscape change detection program developed by the USDA Forest Service. This application is designed to provide a visualization of how vegetation cover, land cover, and land use have changed across US National Forests.

By clicking on a Forest or Forest District boundary, a gif animation will pop up. This animation illustrates how the selected area has changed over the past 37 years.
Click on the animation to open it in a new tab for a larger view.
You can download the gif by right clicking and selecting Save image as..
When any land cover and/or land use map layers are turned on, a time lapse slider will appear in the lower right corner that allows for different years to be shown.
